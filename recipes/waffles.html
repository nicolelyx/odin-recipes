Tender and Easy Buttermilk Waffles

Description

What is better than a big, fluffy waffle first thing in the morning? Almost nothing! These buttermilk waffles are your new favorite breakfast recipeâ€”everyone will love them!

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Salt

Wet ingredients:

  • Buttermilk
  • Melted butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Combine the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients by whisking.
  2. Grease and heat up your waffle iron.
  3. Pour batter into waffle iron.
  4. Cook according to your waffle iron.
